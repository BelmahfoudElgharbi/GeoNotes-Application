GeoNotes


By: Nizar BELMAHFOUD & Mohammed Amine EL GHARBI

During our studies at Telecom Saint-Etienne, we were obliged to do this application as project in the last year of the engineering cycle.

This application is done for only the administrator, who can add notes according to his locations, and he can also delete and modify them.

After adding some notes, the admistrator can define one or lot of paths with the notes wich he has already added before, and those paths will be used by users using another interface.

And finally, we hope that you like our application, enjoy it!!
